A community experiment

Can a community come together to build a good megathread without relying on specific people? We are going to find out. Everything here is open to editing; except the homepage, which is locked to a small group of maintainers to keep the directory clean.

Want in? Open a PR, suggest a page, or fix a typo. Every contribution matters.
